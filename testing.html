What is Testing in Terms of Design Thinking?

Testing is the phase where designers gather feedback on the prototypes from actual users and stakeholders. This step is crucial for evaluating the effectiveness of the solutions and identifying areas for improvement. The feedback loop is essential in design thinking, as it allows for refinement and iteration. Testing may reveal unexpected insights that lead to adjustments in the prototype, and the process continues until a viable and user-approved solution is reached.
