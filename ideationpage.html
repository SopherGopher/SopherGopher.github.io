What is Ideation in Terms of Design Thinking?

Ideation is the creative phase where designers generate a wide range of possible solutions to the defined problem. It's about encouraging free thinking, embracing diversity of thought, and exploring unconventional ideas. Brainstorming sessions, mind maps, and other creative techniques are used to generate a multitude of potential solutions. The goal is to foster creativity and open-mindedness, setting the stage for innovative and unexpected solutions.
