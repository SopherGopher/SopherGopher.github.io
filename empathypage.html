What is Empathy in Terms of Design Thinking?

Empathy is the foundation of design thinking. It involves deeply understanding the needs, motivations, and challenges of the people you are designing for. This goes beyond simply asking users what they want; it requires active listening, observation, and immersing yourself in their experiences. By gaining empathy, designers can develop a more nuanced and accurate understanding of the users' perspectives, which informs the rest of the design process.
