What is Prototyping in Terms of Design Thinking?

Prototyping involves turning selected ideas from the ideation phase into tangible representations. These can range from low-fidelity sketches and wireframes to more high-fidelity models and simulations. The key is to create something that allows for a hands-on, visual, or interactive exploration of the proposed solutions. Prototypes are not final products but serve as a means to test and gather feedback quickly and iteratively.
