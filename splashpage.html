What is Design Thinking?

Design thinking is a user-centric problem-solving approach that integrates empathy, creativity, and collaboration. It involves understanding user needs, defining problems, ideating, prototyping, testing, and iterating to create innovative and effective solutions. The six core elements of design thinking include empathy, defining the problem, ideation, prototyping, testing, and iterative process. By empathizing with users, defining the problem, generating creative ideas, building prototypes, testing with users, and implementing refined solutions, design thinking fosters a dynamic and iterative process for addressing complex challenges and delivering solutions that truly meet user needs.
